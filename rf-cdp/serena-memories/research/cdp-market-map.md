Market map for `cdp`:
- Capture: event tracking, SDKs, forms, surveys.
- Identity/data: identity resolution, customer profiles, CDP, reverse ETL, event routing.
- Activation: marketing automation, email marketing, transactional email, journey builder, lead scoring, personalization.
- Relationship: CRM, customer success, helpdesk, live chat, omnichannel inbox.
- Intelligence: analytics, product analytics, attribution, feature flags, experimentation.
- Orchestration/governance: workflow automation, webhooks, queues, retry/idempotency, multi-tenancy, RBAC, consent, suppression lists, audit logs.

Useful mental model: Website/App -> Tracking -> Event Bus -> Identity Graph -> Unified Profile -> Segments -> Activation -> Channels -> Analytics/Feedback loop.
